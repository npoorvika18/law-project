SECTION: Indian Contract Act Section 10
CATEGORY: Contract Law
KEYWORDS: valid contract, agreement, lawful consideration, free consent

EXPLANATION:
An agreement becomes a valid contract when made with free consent,
lawful consideration, and lawful object.

PUNISHMENT:
Civil compensation for breach.

COURT:
Civil Court

SECTION: Indian Contract Act Sections 13-19
CATEGORY: Contract Law
KEYWORDS: coercion, fraud, undue influence, misrepresentation

EXPLANATION:
Consent obtained through force, fraud, coercion,
or misrepresentation makes contracts voidable.

PUNISHMENT:
Contract may become voidable and compensation may apply.

COURT:
Civil Court
