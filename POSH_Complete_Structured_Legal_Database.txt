
SECTION: POSH Act 2013
CATEGORY: POSH Law
KEYWORDS: workplace harassment, office harassment, sexual harassment, safe workplace, ICC

EXPLANATION:
The POSH Act protects women from sexual harassment at workplaces including offices,
schools, colleges, hospitals, factories, government offices, and online work environments.

PUNISHMENT:
Departmental inquiry, disciplinary action, suspension, termination, or criminal action depending on severity.

BAIL:
Depends on related IPC/BNS offence.

COGNIZABLE:
Depends on related IPC/BNS offence.

COURT:
Internal Complaints Committee (ICC), Labour Court, Magistrate Court

DOCUMENTS:
- Written complaint
- Screenshots/messages
- Witness statements
- HR records

PROCESS:
Complaint → ICC Inquiry → Evidence Collection → Final Report → Employer Action

----------------------------------------------------

SECTION: IPC 354A / BNS
CATEGORY: Sexual Harassment
KEYWORDS: inappropriate touching, sexual remarks, sexual favour, pornography, harassment

EXPLANATION:
Sexual harassment including physical contact, sexual remarks,
demanding sexual favours, or showing pornography.

PUNISHMENT:
Up to 3 years imprisonment and/or fine.

BAIL:
Usually bailable depending on offence.

COGNIZABLE:
Yes

COURT:
Magistrate Court

DOCUMENTS:
- Chat screenshots
- Witness statements
- Audio/video proof

PROCESS:
FIR → Investigation → Chargesheet → Trial

SECTION: IPC 354
CATEGORY: Assault Against Woman
KEYWORDS: assault, criminal force, molestation, physical harassment

EXPLANATION:
Using criminal force or assault against a woman intending to outrage her modesty.

PUNISHMENT:
1 to 5 years imprisonment and fine.

BAIL:
Non-bailable

COGNIZABLE:
Yes

COURT:
Sessions Court / Magistrate Court

DOCUMENTS:

- Witness statements
- CCTV footage

FIR → Medical Examination → Investigation → Court Trial
